About Me

Deshawn Curtis (nicknamed “Shawn” by his family) is very passionate about creating,
and pushing his limits in order to share his art with the world.
Living in Queens, NY his entire life, Deshawn highlights the spot known as “The Rock” which is red,
green, and black representing Black unity. Online, Deshawn runs an art account called “CurtisCreatez”,
which he showcases his art including anime fanart, original pieces, and custom skateboards
through digital art, traditional art, and 3D modeling.
